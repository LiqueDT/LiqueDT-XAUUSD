METHODOLOGY

A narrative compass, not a forecast.

LiqueDT scans recent gold-sensitive headlines for language around the dollar, rates, inflation and risk. It assigns a small directional weight, then aggregates the result into bearish, balanced or bullish narrative context.

Headlines are incomplete, relationships can invert, and price may already reflect the story. Always read the underlying source. This feature must never be used as a standalone reason to trade.
